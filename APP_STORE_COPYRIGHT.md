## Privacy Policy (Required for App Store)

**Blood&Food Privacy Policy**

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

**Data Collection and Storage**

Blood&Food is designed with your privacy in mind. All your health data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information or health data on external servers.

**Information We Don't Collect:**
- Personal health metrics (blood glucose, insulin, meal data)
- Personal identification information
- Usage analytics
- Location data
- Device information

**Data Storage:**
- All data is stored locally on your iOS device
- Data remains under your control at all times
- No data is shared with third parties
- No data is transmitted to external servers

**Your Rights:**
- You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app
- You have complete control over your health information
- No account creation or registration required

**Contact:**
For privacy questions or concerns, please contact Frank Jin.
